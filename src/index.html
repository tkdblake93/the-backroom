Cosmopolitan

Sip on sophistication

Sip on sophistication with our iconic Cosmopolitan. A blend of crisp vodka, zesty lime, and sweet cranberry, balanced with a hint of triple sec, creates this timeless pink-hued masterpiece. Served in a chilled martini glass and garnished to perfection, it's the ultimate cocktail for any occasion.

Daiquiri

Indulge in the classic

Indulge in the classic Daiquiri, a cocktail where simplicity meets elegance. Crafted with the perfect balance of white rum, fresh lime juice, and a whisper of sugar, this drink is a testament to timeless taste. Served shaken and strained into a chilled glass, its crisp, refreshing flavor dances on the palate, offering a burst of citrus with a sweet, smooth finish.

Gimlet

Experience the timeless elegance

Experience the timeless elegance of the Gimlet, a cocktail that perfectly balances the tangy zest of fresh lime with the smooth sophistication of premium gin. First crafted in the early 20th century, the Gimlet has become a symbol of refined simplicity, offering a refreshing taste that is both invigorating and classic.

Manhattan

Indulge in the rich

Indulge in the rich, sophisticated allure of the Manhattan, a cocktail that epitomizes classic elegance and timeless appeal. Crafted with a perfect blend of premium whiskey, sweet vermouth, and a dash of aromatic bitters, the Manhattan offers a harmonious symphony of flavors that dance on the palate. Garnished with a maraschino cherry, this iconic drink is the perfect choice for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Margarita

Dive into the vibrant

Dive into the vibrant, refreshing world of the Margarita, a cocktail that brings a burst of sunshine to any occasion. Featuring a tantalizing blend of premium tequila, freshly squeezed lime juice, and a touch of triple sec, the Margarita strikes the perfect balance between tangy and sweet. Served in a salt-rimmed glass and garnished with a lime wedge, this iconic drink is a celebration of bold flavors and festive spirit.

Negroni 1

Discover the bold sophistication

Discover the bold sophistication of the Negroni, a cocktail that exudes timeless elegance and a perfect balance of flavors. Crafted with equal parts gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth, the Negroni offers a rich, bittersweet taste that is both complex and captivating. Garnished with a twist of orange peel, this iconic Italian aperitif is a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts for its distinctive character and refined simplicity.
